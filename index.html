About

A human behind the infrastructure

My job is to make sure production stays up — and when it doesn't, to understand exactly why and make sure it doesn't happen the same way again. That's the SRE mindset, and it shapes everything I build.

I work primarily in Cloud Operations and SRE, owning on-call rotations, incident response, and post-mortem culture. Alongside that I do some DevOps work leveraging DevOps tools — CI/CD pipelines, automation scripts, and infrastructure improvements that reduce the toil my team deals with every week.

I care about the feedback loop between development and operations. The best way to reduce incidents is to involve ops thinking early — in architecture reviews, in deployment design, in how alerts are written. I push for that wherever I work.

What I bring to a team
  • On-call ownership — I take incidents seriously and drive them to resolution
  • Post-mortem culture — blameless, thorough, and actually followed up on
  • Toil reduction — I automate the repetitive ops work that burns teams out
  • Observability thinking — alerts that wake you up for the right reasons
